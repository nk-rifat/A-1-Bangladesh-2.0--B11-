Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation transforms ideas into creative solutions that drive progress and inspire change

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures balance, preserving resources for future generations through mindful, responsible actions.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure builds strong foundations, connecting communities and enabling growth for a better future.

Empowerment

Empowerment unlocks potential, fostering confidence, growth, and positive change in individuals' lives

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Poverty is not a natural condition; it’s a product of systems we’ve built, but systems can be redesigned. Through innovation, kindness, and collective action, we can create a world where poverty no longer exists. Microcredit has shown us that even the smallest resources,when placed in the right hands, can unlock unimaginable potential.When we believe in people and empower them with the tools they need, we spark a cycle of transformation. Empowerment fosters hope,hope drives action, and action creates lasting change. The fight against poverty isn’t about charity alone; it’s about justice, opportunity, and dismantling the barriers that hold people back.Each of us holds the power to make a difference—through our choices, voices, and actions. Together, we can build a future where everyone has the chance to thrive. Let’s act wisely and boldly, for a better brighter tomorrow.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
